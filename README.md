## About Me

I am a software engineer with nearly three years of experience building and supporting applications used by teams across accounting, treasury, transactions and settlement, business operations, and back-office functions.

Working closely with different departments has given me a strong understanding of how each part of the business contributes to the bigger picture. That perspective helps me build software that is not only functional, but also practical, maintainable, scalable, and aligned with real business needs.

I enjoy building systems that quietly do their job well. When an API or backend service is stable, fast, and dependable, it fades into the background and lets the business move without friction.

Right now, I am focused on cloud-native solutions, infrastructure as code, practical AI and LLM integrations, and strengthening my fundamentals across the full software development lifecycle.

***I like building software that feels invisible when it is working well and dependable when it matters most.***
